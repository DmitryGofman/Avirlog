# AvirLog — Privacy Policy

_Last updated: June 2026_

AvirLog is a breath-awareness journal. We designed it to be private by default.

## What we collect

Nothing is sent to us. AvirLog has no account and no server in this version.
Everything you log — your breath states, optional mood/energy/focus scores,
notes, tags, and settings — is stored **only on your device**.

## What we do not collect

- We do not collect personal information.
- We do not use analytics or tracking.
- We do not use advertising or third-party SDKs that collect data.
- We do not share or sell any data.

## Notifications

If you enable reminders, AvirLog schedules **local** notifications on your
device. They are generated on-device and are not routed through any server.

## Your data and control

Because your data lives only on your device:

- You can export it at any time from **Settings → Export data**.
- Deleting the app removes all of your data.

## Children

AvirLog is not directed to children under 13.

## Health disclaimer

AvirLog provides educational content based on Swara Yoga tradition and general
wellness research. It is **not medical advice** and is not intended to
diagnose, treat, or prevent any condition.

## Changes

If a future version adds optional account sign-in or cloud sync, this policy
will be updated to describe what that involves, and such features will be
opt-in.
